Proyecto de realidad virtual

The simulation begins for the users in their virtual home: First, they have to pack all the necessary items for a tour in the mountains - including an avalanche transceiver, shovel and probe - into their backpack. Once this is done, the ascent to the alpine area follows. After the avalanche transceiver check and emergency briefing, the mountain tour is started, during which an avalanche is assumed to have occurred. In the simulation, a buried person has to be located. To do this, various techniques such as avalanche transceiver search and point location/probing have to be used. The scenario finally ends with the shovelling out and the arrival of the professional rescue forces.
