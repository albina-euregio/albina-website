Forest tracks

The forest track status map is updated weekly on Fridays and when conditions change significantly (snowfalls, etc.). The “open, chains and closed” category at each point refers to the conditions of the section that follows.

ATTENTION: DRIVING ON FOREST TRACKS DURING THE WINTER AND SPRING MONTHS IS THE PERSONAL RESPONSIBILITY OF DRIVERS. THIS PRACTICE REQUIRES EXPERIENCE IN ASSESSING CONDITIONS AND IN DRIVING. IT IS ADVISABLE TO BRING SNOW TIRES, BE EQUIPPED WITH CHAINS, INFORM SOMEONE OF YOUR ROUTE AND AVOID GOING ALONE.

THIS INFORMATION IS FOR GUIDANCE ONLY. LOCALLY WE MAY FIND ICE SHEETS, TREES, ROCKS, ETC. THAT COMPROMISE OUR SAFETY AND ARE NOT SHOWN ON THIS MAP.

Information provided by the Environmental Guards of the Conselh Generau d'Aran.
