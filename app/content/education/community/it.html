What is this all about?

The avalanche warning services of Tyrol, South Tyrol, and Trentino are starting a research initiative to better understand how different types of backcountry users and decision-makers use their avalanche report products. A better understanding of usersâ€™ needs and challenges is critical for improving our existing products and developing new ones.

While we are developing our report feedback survey, we are current looking for winter backcountry users (e.g., backcountry skiers/snowboarders, snow shoers, ice climbers, etc.) and community decision-makers (e.g. local avalanche commissions) interested in being part of a growing community that the avalanche warning services can consult to get feedback on their products and brainstorm about new opportunities.

Please note that you do not have to be a long-term backcountry recreationists or avalanche expert to participate in our research. We are interested in the perspectives of everybody, particularly individuals who are just starting their backcountry adventures or only occasionally go into the backcountry.

If you are interested in participating in this initiative, please click the 'Sign me up' button below to provide us with a bit of background information about yourself. We will contact you are soon as our first survey is ready.
