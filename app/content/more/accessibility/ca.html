Declaració d'accessibilitat

D’acord amb la Decisió d’Execució (UE) 2018/1523 de la Comissió d’11 d’octubre 2018 establint un model de declaració d’accessibilitat d’acord amb Directiva (UE) 2016/2102 del Parlament Europeu i del Consell sobre accés sense barreres als llocs web i aplicacions mòbils de públic organismes sectorials.

Declaració sobre l'accessibilitat

L’Oficina del Govern Regional del Tirol s’esforça a aconseguir el seu lloc web accessible d’acord amb l’apartat 14b de la Llei Tirolesa d'antidiscriminació de 2005 que implementa la Directiva (UE) 2016/2102 de la Parlament Europeu i del Consell, del 26 d’octubre de 2016, d'accés sense barreres als llocs web i a les aplicacions mòbils dels organismes del sector públic (OJ L 327, 2.12.2016, pàg. 1).

Aquesta declaració d’accessibilitat s’aplica a "Avalanche.report" | "Avalanche.report" | "Valanghe.report".

Estat de compatibilitat amb els requisits

Aquest lloc web compleix parcialment el nivell de conformitat AA del "Web " Directrius d'accessibilitat - WCAG 2.1 "segons el que correspongui amb la norma europea harmonitzada "Norma europea EN 301 549 V2.1.2 (2018-08) "a causa de les incompatibilitats següents.

Contingut no accessible

The content listed below is not accessible for the following reasons:

1. Incompatibility with accessibility regulations.
1.1 Perceptibility

For some images in blog entries the alternative text is missing, so this information is not accessible for screen reader users. This means that WCAG success criterion 1.1.1 (non-text content) is not met. All new images are published together with alternative text.

Some pages are missing level 2, 3, 4 and 5 headings, thus WCAG success criteria 1.3.1 (info and relationships) is not met. We plan to fix the problem in 2021.

On some pages, the minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 of text and images of text is not met. This means that WCAG success criterion 1.3.3 Contrast (minimum) is not met.

Images and graphics are used to provide weather and climate content in a compact and effective way. For technical reasons and due to the high update rates of the files to be visualised, we are not able to design all illustrations and graphics in an accessible and barrier-free manner and in these cases do not fulfil success criterion 1.1.1 (text alternative). This includes animated weather forecasts and analyses.

2. Disproportionate burden

Our videos do not have audio description. This means that WCAG success criterion 1.2.5 (audio description recorded) is not met. The content of the video is available in textual form directly with the videos.

Enforcement procedure

If you do not receive satisfactory answers from the above-mentioned contact option, you can turn to the Ombudsman's Office for Accessible Internet and Mobile Applications of the Province of Tyrol by means of a complaint. The complaint will be examined to determine whether there has been a violation of the provisions of § 14b of the Tyrolean Anti-Discrimination Act by organs of the province, the municipalities, the associations of municipalities and the self-governing bodies established by provincial law. If the complaint is justified, the ombudsperson's office must make recommendations to the province or the legal entities concerned and propose measures to remedy the deficiencies. Further information on the complaints procedure can be found on the website of the Ombudsman's Office for Accessible Internet and Mobile Applications.
